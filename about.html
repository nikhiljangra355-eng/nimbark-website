About Nimbark Research Insights

A trusted partner for research scholars across India — built on academic ethics, subject expertise and genuine care for every scholar's success.

Our Story

Nimbark Research Insights was founded in Kurukshetra — the historic seat of learning and home to Kurukshetra University — with a simple observation: research scholars in India work incredibly hard, yet many struggle alone at critical stages of their journey. A synopsis returned by the Departmental Research Committee, a dataset that refuses to make sense, a journal rejection with cryptic reviewer comments, a plagiarism report that arrives too close to the submission date — these moments can stall a research career for months.

We built Nimbark to be the experienced colleague every scholar deserves. What began as informal guidance for a few research scholars has grown into a full-service research support organisation assisting PhD, MPhil and Master's scholars from central, state, deemed and private universities across India — every one of them supported with the same care, whatever their institution or discipline.

Today, our team of subject-matter experts, statisticians, academic editors and publication consultants works with scholars at every stage of the research journey — from the first synopsis to the final journal publication — while holding firmly to the principle that our role is to assist, educate and strengthen the scholar's own work, never to replace it.

Our Mission

To provide a trusted global platform for academic excellence, ethical publishing, and impactful research dissemination.

Our Vision

To become India's most respected research support organisation — one where every scholar, whatever their university, subject or background, can access the expertise, tools and mentorship needed to produce research they are genuinely proud of.

Our Values

  • Academic integrity first. We work within university and UGC guidelines. Our assistance is educational and supportive — we help you understand and improve your work, and every deliverable is original and plagiarism-checked.
  • Absolute confidentiality. Your identity, topic, data and documents are never shared. A non-disclosure agreement is available to every client on request.
  • Subject-matter depth. Your work is handled by experts from your own discipline — commerce, management, education, law, social sciences, sciences, engineering and more — not by generalist writers.
  • Transparent communication. Clear scope, clear timelines, staged delivery and honest answers — including telling you when something is not achievable in your timeframe.
  • Scholar success over sales. We measure ourselves by approvals, acceptances and completed degrees, not by the number of projects we sign.

Why Scholars Choose Us

  • End-to-end support: topic selection, synopsis, thesis chapters, data analysis, plagiarism removal, publication, book publishing and even patents — one accountable team for the entire journey.
  • University-format compliance: we work to your university's exact guidelines, citation style and evaluation norms, which is why our synopses and theses clear committees smoothly.
  • Real statistical expertise: hands-on analysts working in SPSS, AMOS, R and STATA who can also sit with you and explain every output before your presentation or viva.
  • Revisions included: supervisor feedback is part of research — the number of revision rounds is agreed before work begins, and revisions within that agreement and the project scope are free, never billed as surprise extras.
  • Proof, not promises: every written deliverable ships with a plagiarism similarity report.
  • Local and reachable: visit our Kurukshetra office opposite the 3rd Gate of Kurukshetra University, or reach us any day on phone and WhatsApp — you always talk to a real person who knows your file.

Our Commitment to Ethical Assistance

Research support is meaningful only when it strengthens genuine scholarship. That is why we insist on working with scholars: we expect your inputs, share drafts in stages, explain our reasoning, and encourage you to defend every page as your own understanding. Our goal is that you walk into your pre-submission seminar or viva more confident about your research than ever before.
