Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Last updated: July 2026

Nimbark Research Insights ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through www.nimbarkinsights.com and our consultation process, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you submit through our enquiry forms, WhatsApp, phone or email — your name, phone number and email address.
  • Research information you choose to share — your subject, topic, university requirements, drafts and related documents.
  • Basic technical data collected automatically when you browse the site (such as pages visited and device type) if analytics is enabled — used only in aggregate to improve the website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the research support services you request.
  • To send you updates about your own project and, occasionally, information about our services.
  • To improve our website and service quality.

3. Confidentiality of Your Research

Your identity, topic and documents are treated as strictly confidential. Access is limited to the team members working on your project. We are happy to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) on request. We never publish, resell or reuse your research material.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data is shared only with the service providers that make our website work (such as secure form storage and email delivery), and only to the extent needed to deliver our services, or where disclosure is required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Enquiry data is stored on secured, access-controlled systems. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, alteration or loss.

6. Your Rights

You may ask us at any time to review, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you. Write to info@nimbarkinsights.com and we will act on your request promptly.

For any privacy-related question, contact us at info@nimbarkinsights.com, call 95881-42496, or visit us at #1640/12, Opp. 3rd Gate Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra, Haryana, India.
