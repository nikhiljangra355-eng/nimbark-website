Turnaround: 4–8 weeks ISBN registration included Author keeps full copyright

Your Thesis Can Reach Far Beyond the Examination Panel

After years of work, most theses are read by exactly three people: the supervisor and two examiners. Publishing your research as a book changes that — it becomes citable scholarship available to students and researchers everywhere, a powerful line on your academic CV, and a recognised contribution under UGC's academic performance indicators for career advancement. For many scholars, a published book is also the most satisfying way to close the PhD chapter of their lives.

But a thesis is not yet a book. Examiners want exhaustive methodology and literature coverage; readers want a clear narrative. Our publishing team handles the complete transformation: restructuring chapters for readers, trimming examination scaffolding, professional editing, page design, cover creation, ISBN registration, printing and online availability — while you retain 100% of the copyright and authorship.

What's Included

  • Thesis-to-book restructuring: reader-friendly chapter flow, refreshed introduction and conclusion
  • Professional copy editing and proofreading of the full manuscript
  • Interior page layout and typesetting (print and digital editions)
  • Custom cover design — three concepts, unlimited refinement of your chosen one
  • ISBN registration in your name as the author
  • Print production: quality paperback/hardcover copies delivered to your address
  • Online listing on Amazon and Flipkart, plus a digital (e-book) edition on request
  • Author copies and a marketing kit: book description, author bio and launch announcement content

Our Process

  1. Manuscript review: we assess your thesis or manuscript and agree the book's scope, format and edition plan.
  2. Restructure and edit: the manuscript is transformed and polished, with your approval at each stage.
  3. Design: you approve the interior layout and choose from three cover concepts.
  4. ISBN and production: registration, final print files, and a printed proof copy for your sign-off.
  5. Release: printing, delivery of author copies, and online store listings go live.

Turnaround Expectations

A standard thesis-to-book project completes in 4–8 weeks from manuscript handover: editing and restructuring take 2–4 weeks, design and your approvals 1–2 weeks, and ISBN, printing and listing 1–2 weeks. Edited volumes and textbooks are scoped individually.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who owns the copyright of the published book?

You do — fully and permanently. The ISBN is registered with you as the author, the copyright page carries your name, and you are free to reprint, revise or republish anywhere in future. We are a publishing service, not a rights-taking publisher.

Will publishing my thesis as a book create plagiarism problems?

No — you are the author of both works, and university repositories expect this practice. We still restructure and rewrite substantially (a book reads very differently from a thesis) and advise on your university's specific policy about prior publication if you haven't yet submitted.

Can I publish only part of my research, or with co-authors?

Yes. Monographs from selected chapters, co-authored volumes with your supervisor, and edited books collecting several scholars' papers are all common formats we handle. Authorship order and credits are set exactly as you specify.
