Publish Where It Counts

For Indian research scholars, publication is no longer optional. Most universities require at least one publication in a UGC-CARE listed journal before thesis submission, and academic careers increasingly depend on Scopus and Web of Science indexed publications for recruitment, promotion under UGC regulations, and research credibility. Yet the publication landscape is a minefield: cloned journals, predatory publishers charging for worthless "acceptance letters", and legitimate journals that desk-reject the majority of submissions for avoidable presentation problems.

Our publication team keeps current with the UGC-CARE list and the Scopus and Web of Science databases, and — just as important — knows the warning signs of predatory outlets. We match your manuscript to genuine, active, correctly indexed journals in your field, prepare it to the journal's exact specifications, and stay with you through every round of peer review until the paper is accepted.

What's Included

  • Journal shortlist: 3–5 verified journals matched to your scope, timeline and indexing requirement
  • Verification of indexing status (live UGC-CARE / Scopus / Web of Science checks) so you never fall for a cloned journal
  • Formatting to the journal's author guidelines — structure, references, tables, figures, word limits
  • Cover letter, author declarations, and plagiarism check before submission
  • Submission handling and status tracking on the journal portal
  • Point-by-point responses to reviewer comments and revised manuscript preparation
  • Support until final decision — including resubmission strategy if a journal rejects

Our Process

  1. Manuscript review: we read your paper and give a frank readiness report with a recommended target tier.
  2. Journal selection: you approve the target journal from our verified shortlist.
  3. Preparation: formatting, language polish, plagiarism check, cover letter.
  4. Submission and tracking: we submit, monitor, and keep you informed at every status change.
  5. Peer-review support: we draft revision responses with you until acceptance.

Turnaround Expectations

Manuscript preparation takes 1–2 weeks. After submission, timelines belong to the journal: UGC-CARE journals typically decide in 2–6 months; Scopus, SCI and Web of Science journals in 4–12 months including revision rounds. Beware of anyone promising guaranteed Scopus acceptance in weeks — that is the signature of a predatory operation. We give you realistic timelines and genuine indexing, verified on the day of submission.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you guarantee publication?

No honest service can guarantee a peer-reviewed acceptance — the decision belongs to independent reviewers. What we guarantee is our process: genuine indexed journals, correct preparation, and persistent revision support until acceptance. This is why our supported papers have a strong acceptance record.

How do I know a journal is genuinely UGC-CARE or Scopus indexed?

We verify every journal against the live UGC-CARE portal and the official Scopus source list on the day of submission, and show you the verification. Cloned journals with copied names and fake websites are common — read our guide on UGC-CARE vs Scopus to understand the checks yourself.

My paper was rejected. Can you still help?

Yes — rejection is normal, even for good research. We analyse the rejection letter, strengthen whatever the reviewers criticised (framing, literature, analysis or language), and resubmit to a better-matched journal. Many of our successful publications began as rejections elsewhere.
